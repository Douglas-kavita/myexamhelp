## Welcome

MyExamHelp exists to help students and professionals with exams, assignments, and online assessments.

### How to get help quickly
- Send the task instructions
- Share the deadline
- Mention the platform (Google Forms, Moodle, Blackboard, etc.)
- Attach screenshots/files where possible
